Things to do in Clarens

  • Clarens Brewery

    A staple in Clarens. Enjoy a beer, cider or gin tasting at Clarens Brewery.

  • Golden Gate National Park

    Situated 18km from Clarens is the beautiful Golden Gate Highlands National Park. The park offers visitors plenty to see and do.

  • Baris Monger

    Voted the best cheesecake, Baris Monger offers a selection of cheeses, ice-cream and meats all made on the farm.

  • Dine at local restaurants

    Some of our faves:

    The Platform

    Clementines

    Mosaic

    Bocca Di Lupo

  • Hiking

    For the outdoors-ey types, the Clarens Conservancy offers a scenic hike. There are several trails to choose from.
